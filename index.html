लिपि — The Script

Learn to read and write Devanagari, the script used to write Hindi.

स्वर — Vowels

Hindi has 12 vowels. Click a card to flip it and see the pronunciation and example. Use the Pronounce button to hear the sound.

व्यंजन — Consonants

Hindi consonants are organized by where in the mouth the sound is made. Flip each card to see transliteration and examples.

मात्राएँ — Vowel Signs

When a vowel follows a consonant, it's written as a matra (symbol) attached to the consonant, not as a standalone letter.
